## Brilliant products fail without a path to revenue.

Research teams, startups, and established manufacturers develop ground-breaking medical technologies — then falter when it comes time to sell. The gap between a working product and a paying customer is where most health technology ventures lose momentum.

The challenge is rarely the technology itself. It is navigating hospital procurement, building reimbursement pathways, and finding the right commercial model for a complex, risk-averse market.

## We work backwards from the sale.

Digital Health Works starts where it matters most: who will buy your product, how they will pay for it, and what it takes to close the deal. We call this approach Value Dynamics — a methodology that maps the revenue pathway before you go to market.

From there, we build the commercial infrastructure around your innovation. Distribution channels, hospital relationships, pricing models, and direct sales — all grounded in the economics of healthcare, not assumptions.

The result is a product that reaches clinicians, improves patient outcomes, and generates sustainable revenue.

## What We Do

Three integrated capabilities — health economics, commercial strategy, and distributor sales — that take your medical technology from validated concept to revenue.

### Value Dynamics

Health Economics & Value Strategy

We analyze the economic, clinical, and institutional forces that determine whether your medical technology gets adopted. Starting from the point of sale and working backwards, we map reimbursement pathways, model budget impact, identify the stakeholders who control purchasing decisions, and build the value framework that procurement committees and payers need to say yes.

### Market Development

Commercial Strategy & Market Entry

We translate the financial understanding from Value Dynamics into a concrete commercial strategy. This means designing how the money needs to flow — structuring pricing models, building distribution channels, identifying the right partners, and preparing the evidence packages that hospital value analysis committees require. The result is a go-to-market roadmap with clear milestones from market entry through sustainable revenue.

### Distributor Sales

Manufacturer's Sales Representation

We act as your sales team in the US and European markets. As a manufacturer's sales representative, we build pipeline through distribution partners, engage hospital and health system decision-makers, navigate procurement, close contracts, and manage ongoing customer relationships — driving measurable revenue growth with deep domain expertise in medtech and digital health.
