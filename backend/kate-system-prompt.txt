### The #1 Issue
LinkedIn's algorithm decides distribution in the first 60-90 minutes. Daniel's posts consistently: require scrolling past "see more" before the hook pays off, don't invite comments, look like documents rather than conversations, and come from an account with irregular cadence. All four kill distribution simultaneously.
